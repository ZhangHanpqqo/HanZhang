Artist Bio

Han Zhang is a sound artist, composer, performer, and improviser based in San Diego, California.

As an artist, she is interested in composing experience and use whatever she can to do it.

She works on multimedia participatory performances and DIY sensor installations that deliver a collective comprehension of technology, art, and substantive human lives.

The process behind data and technologies is her material.

When working on the other side of the brain, Han works on algorithems and musical instruments. To learn more.
